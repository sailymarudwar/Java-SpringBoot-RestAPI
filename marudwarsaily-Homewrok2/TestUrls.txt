I have used RequestParam for the Part 1.1 where we have to find Employees by SID and Position

to get this mapping, you have to pass ?sid=xxxxx&pos=x-x

Following are the four sample URLs for the four parts of 1.1, you can replace the SIDs and Positions to test these:

http://localhost:8081/employeesbySidAndPos?sid=91254&pos=E-2
http://localhost:8081/employeesbySidAndPos?sid=91254
http://localhost:8081/employeesbySidAndPos?pos=E-2
http://localhost:8081/employeesbySidAndPos
